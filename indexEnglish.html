About us

Who are we?

TRIO export-import, a. s., was founded in 2005. By continuously expanding the range of protective work gloves and introducing new products, the company pursues the possibility of assembling a variant offer ranging from economical designs to high-end models corresponding to the latest trends in the development of personal protective equipment.

Sortiment

Personal protective equipment is a specific product range that is supplied in accordance with the relevant standards. On aero-gloves.com you can find a wide range of protective work gloves.

We are keen to help our customers to secure goods that are not included in our standard glove portfolio. Customer satisfaction is our primary concern. Our team strives to achieve this goal primarily through their own professionalism and individual approach.
